Beef Bourguignon Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds (900g) beef stew meat, cut into chunks
  • 4 slices bacon, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 cups red wine (Burgundy wine recommended)
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 pound (450g) mushrooms, quartered
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large Dutch oven, cook the chopped bacon over medium heat until crispy. Remove the bacon and set it aside.
  2. Season the beef chunks with salt and black pepper. Add olive oil to the Dutch oven and brown the beef in batches. Remove the beef and set it aside.
  3. Add chopped onion, minced garlic, and sliced carrots to the Dutch oven. Sauté until the onions are translucent.
  4. Stir in the tomato paste and cook for a minute.
  5. Return the beef and bacon to the pot. Pour in the red wine and beef broth. Add dried thyme and bay leaves. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 2 hours, or until the beef is tender.
  6. In a separate skillet, sauté the mushrooms in a little olive oil until they're browned and tender.
  7. Remove the bay leaves from the beef mixture and discard them.
  8. Combine flour and some of the cooking liquid from the Dutch oven to create a smooth paste. Stir it back into the pot to thicken the sauce.
  9. Add the sautéed mushrooms to the pot and simmer for an additional 10-15 minutes.
  10. Adjust the seasoning with salt and black pepper to taste.
  11. Serve your delicious Beef Bourguignon hot, garnished with chopped fresh parsley.
